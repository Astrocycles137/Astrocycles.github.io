Privacy Policy for Astrocycles

Last Updated: [28/09/2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Astrocycles, you may provide the following information:

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Time of birth
  • Location of birth
  • Optional partner information (name, date, time, location of birth)

Important: All information you provide is stored only on your device. Astrocycles does not collect, transmit, or store this information on external servers.

2. How Your Information Is Used

The information you enter is used solely within the App to generate personalized astrology charts and interpretations. We do not access, share, or upload this information.

3. Advertising

Astrocycles displays third-party advertisements to support the App:

  • Ads are provided by external ad networks (such as Google AdMob).
  • Currently, these ads are served using generic, non-personalized data.
  • In the future, personalized ads may be introduced. If so, ad networks may use device identifiers or app interactions to serve more relevant ads, in line with their own privacy policies and applicable consent requirements.
  • Astrocycles itself does not collect or control this data.

For details on how ad providers handle information, please review their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

Astrocycles does not sell, rent, or share your personal information. Because all astrology data is stored locally on your device, it is never accessible to the developer. The only information that may be processed comes from third-party advertising networks as described above.

5. Children’s Privacy

Astrocycles is not directed toward children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

Since your astrology data remains on your device, you maintain control over its security. We recommend using device security features (such as passcodes or biometrics) to protect your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Effective Date.”
