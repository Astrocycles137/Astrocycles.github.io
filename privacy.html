Privacy Policy for Astrocycles

Last Updated: 12/10/2025

Astrocycles (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app processes locally on your device, what limited data may be collected by third-party advertising partners, and your choices.

1) Information You Enter (Stored Only on Your Device)

Astrocycles is designed so that your astrology data remains on your device. We do not transmit it to our servers.

  • Name
  • Date/time of birth
  • Place of birth
  • Optional partner info (name, date/time/place of birth)
  • Optional device location (for moon/sun times & local sky info)
  • Journal entries or notes

How it’s used: Solely to generate charts, sky windows, and interpretations inside the app.

1.1 Optional Device Location Use

Astrocycles may request optional foreground device location (GPS or network-based) to automatically calculate accurate astronomical data such as sunrise, moonrise, and current planet visibility. This permission is:

  • Optional – You may deny location and still use the app.
  • Foreground-only – The app does not access location in the background.
  • Local-only – Your location is not sent to our servers or stored by us.
  • Purpose-limited – Used only for sky calculations and shown inside the app.

If you prefer, you may manually search for a town or location instead of enabling device location. You can disable location access anytime in your Android system settings.

2) Advertising & Third-Party Data

Astrocycles displays ads using the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). We do not run our own analytics or back-end collection of your astrology inputs. However, AdMob may collect certain device and app-interaction data to serve and measure ads.

What AdMob may collect

  • Identifiers: Android Advertising ID (AAID) or similar.
  • Approximate location (derived from IP) for regional ad compliance.
  • App interactions: ad impressions, clicks.
  • Device & diagnostics: error logs, OS version.

Do We Share or Sell Your Location?

No. If you grant location access, Astrocycles uses it only on your device and never sells or shares GPS location with third parties. AdMob may infer approximate region from IP for ad delivery, but we do not provide your GPS location to AdMob.

Appendix: Data Safety Summary (Google Play)

Collected by Astrocycles (developer): None. Astrology inputs are processed and stored locally only.

Optional device location (device GPS): Processed locally only when enabled by user. Not transmitted or shared.

Collected by third parties (AdMob):

  • Approximate location (IP-based only) – ads delivery/compliance
  • Device or other IDs (AAID) – ads measurement
  • App interactions – ads measurement
  • Diagnostics – fraud prevention
